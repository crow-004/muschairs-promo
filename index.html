About The Game

Musical Chairs is a live Web3 game on the Arbitrum network. It sidesteps complex tokenomics for a simple, fast-paced skill game with a zero-sum, player-funded economy.

The concept is simple: 5 chairs. 4 winners. 1 shot.

Inspired by the rush for seats on the Dubai Metro, we've built a transparent, on-chain experience where timing is everything. Featuring low latency mechanics and direct smart-contract interaction, it demonstrates the speed of modern L2s.

Why Play?

  • Fair: Fully on-chain logic.
  • Fast: Built on Arbitrum for speed and low fees.
  • Profitable: Win ETH directly from other players.
